Key Insights
Gender (28.1% importance)
The most powerful predictor. Women had a 71% survival rate vs 28.4% for men, driven by evacuation protocol "women and children first."
Fare / Wealth (22.0% importance)
Ticket price is a strong proxy for socioeconomic status. Higher fare → upper decks → closer to lifeboats → better chance of survival.
Age (12.7% importance)
Children under 12 benefited from evacuation priority. Elderly passengers (60+) had slightly lower survival rates due to mobility limitations.
Passenger Class (8.8% importance)
1st class passengers (71.6% survival) had cabins near lifeboats. 3rd class (29.3%) were below the waterline and faced locked gates.
